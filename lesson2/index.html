White Space

Dell

dell.com

In this homepage for the company Dell we see white space being used to make the content readable to the user. If we look at the click boxes on the page we see that the words inside them have a fair amount of padding around them to separate them from their container. We can also see that other content on the page, such as the laptop image, are literally surrounded by white space so that the page doesn't feel cluttered.

Alignment

Dell

dell.com

This page does a great job in keeping things aligned as we see at the center of the page. From the click boxes that say "Learn More" and "Show Now" all the way up to the image of the laptop, everything seems to be centered by invisible lines from the left and right sides.

Contrast

Oracle

oracle.com

On this homepage for Oracle we see contrast in the colors that are used throughout the page. In this screenshot we see contrast between the two options in the background color used for the "Applications" and "Infrastructure" options. The white font also makes the text more readable within their respective containers.
