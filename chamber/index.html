"Restaurant of the Week" Launches in ELA

As a way to support our local restaurants, this new initiative will highlight a different restaurant each week on the city website. Community members will be able to get to know the people behind the food and identify their next great culinary experience. Food brings people of all backgrounds together, and we know that our city hosts some of the best and diverse foods found in the county. Let's highlight your restaurant next!
