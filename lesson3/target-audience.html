Description

The target audience are business owners in the community who are looking to find a place where they can better meet the needs of their business. The resources they are looking for are quick to find on the site. General audience characteristics are those between 35-50 years old with families and a small business.

Personas

Angela Ruiz

Angela has lived in the community for many years and owns a small breakfast restaurant that serves Mexican food. This business has helped her be a major provider for her family. She is currently 45 years old and has two children. Lately she feels her business has fallen behind as more big chains have moved into the neighborhood. Now she is looking for ways to better reach her customers and remain a well known restaurant in the area.

Ruben Santos

Ruben is 30 years old and has finally been able to open his own restaurant selling Salvadorian food. He has put in a lot of hard work and his business is doing well in the neighborhood. Now he wants to get more involved in the community and see what he can do to better his business image.

Scenarios

1. What discounts can I get for my business?

2. What events can by business join?

3. How do I contact the chamber of commerce?

4. What other businesses are in the area?

5. How can the chamber of commerce help my business?
